Harry Spotter

About

Harry Spotter is location based AR game, Nima Jamalian internship project in Nokia Bell Labs UK.

The goal of the app is to make crowd sourcing more fun by using gamification elements üéÆ in the app users need to take picture from objects around them and label them. This help us to understand how we has humans view the objects around us. üåé

How it work!

  • When you first open the application you need to create an account.
  • Then you answer series of personality questions, based on your answer you get assign into one of the Harry Potter houses!
  • You gain point in the game by taking picture of object üì∑ and labelling them,
  • Your points will help you team in the leaderboard and you can use to take territories for your team!
  • In the main screen of the app, you see a map and there would be highlighted location near you. You can take over these locations and help you team!
  • Once you are in close range of highlighted location, you can fight the AR wizard which will appear in front of you! üßô
